Meetups That We Dig

DUX: Downtown UX

Their mission is to create opportunities for anyone to learn, discuss, and share knowledge about the field of user experience.

Orlando Tech

Orlando Tech is a community-built organization that connects, promotes and fosters the growth of technology companies in Orlando.

Design Orlando

Design discussions on the second Tuesday of every month. Many of the topics will be focused around the web, but we are open to discussing any type of design.
